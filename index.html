Introduction to Kyber Crystals

Kyber crystals are rare, Force-attuned crystals that are used to power lightsabers and other devices.

Main Kyber Crystal Types

Known in ancient times as a kaiburr crystal, it was used by both the Jedi and the Sith in the construction of their lightsabers. Kyber crystals come in a variety of colors, each with its own significance and meaning.

Through an unspecified process involving being placed in a furnace and hammering, kyber crystals could be tempered to match with the nature of multiple individuals multiple times, beside bonding with just one for life. When these tempered crystals are placed within a weapon such as a lightsaber, its color will change within moments upon being picked up by a new wielder.

Blue Kyber Crystal

The blue kyber crystal is associated with the Jedi Guardian path.

Green Kyber Crystal

The green kyber crystal is associated with the Jedi Consular path.

Purple Kyber Crystal

The purple kyber crystal is associated with the Jedi Sentinel path.

Yellow Kyber Crystal

The yellow kyber crystal is associated with the Temple Guards.

White Kyber Crystal

The white kyber crystal is associated with the light side of the Force.

Orange Kyber Crystal

The orange kyber crystal is associated with the light side of the Force.

Gray Kyber Crystal

The gray kyber crystal is associated with the light side of the Force.

Black Kyber Crystal

The black kyber crystal is associated with the Darksaber.

Red Kyber Crystal

The red kyber crystal is associated with the Sith.

There is only one planet where you find kyber crystals used to make lightsabres, a planet in the Unknown Regions of the galaxy, known for its icy surface and ancient Jedi temples.

Ilum

Ilum is the primary source of kyber crystals in the galaxy, and it is where Jedi younglings sent to find their first lightsaber crystals, saying that the crystals sing to them.
